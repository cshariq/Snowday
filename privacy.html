Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is Sapphire's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Sapphire.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Sapphire operates entirely locally on your computer. Features that require internet access, such as the weather widget, only communicate with third-party APIs to fetch data and do not send any personal information from your device.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use it for any purpose. Any data processed by the application (like your calendar events or location for weather) remains on your device and is subject to the privacy policies of the services you connect (e.g., Apple Calendar).

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. The app is open-source, and you can inspect the code yourself to verify our privacy claims.

4. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Since all data is stored and processed locally, the security of your information is handled by your operating system's built-in security features.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by email at shariq.charolia@gmail.com.
