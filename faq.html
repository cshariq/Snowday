Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out.

What is Sapphire?

Sapphire is a macOS application designed to enhance the functionality of the notch on modern MacBooks. It transforms the notch area into a dynamic, interactive space for widgets like music controls, weather updates, calendar events, and more.

Is Sapphire free to use?

Yes, Sapphire is completely free and open-source. You can download it, use it, and even contribute to its development on GitHub.

Which versions of macOS are supported?

Sapphire is designed for MacBooks with a notch and officially supports macOS Sonoma (14.0) and later. While it might run on older versions, we recommend the latest OS for the best performance and all features.

How do I install Sapphire?

Simply download the latest release from our website or the GitHub releases page. Unzip the file, and drag the `Sapphire.app` file into your Applications folder. That's it!

Does Sapphire collect any personal data?

No. Sapphire is designed with privacy as a priority. The app runs entirely locally on your machine and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

How does the "Nearby Share" feature work?

The Nearby Share feature is an upcoming integration that will allow you to seamlessly share files, links, and photos from your Android device directly to your Mac, similar to AirDrop. This feature is currently in development.
